About Uganda

Uganda is a beautiful country in East Africa known for its diverse wildlife, friendly people, and natural beauty. It is home to Lake Victoria, mountain gorillas, and stunning landscapes.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Kampala
  • Population: 45 Million+
  • Language: English, Luganda
  • Currency: Ugandan Shilling
