Welcome to the Pearl of Africa

Uganda is one of the most biodiverse countries on Earth. From the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains to the vast savannas of Queen Elizabeth National Park, from the source of the world's longest river to misty rainforests sheltering mountain gorillas — Uganda offers adventures found nowhere else.

Winston Churchill called it the "Pearl of Africa" for good reason: its magnificent scenery, friendly people, and incredible wildlife make it an unforgettable destination.

Why Visit Uganda?

🦍 Mountain Gorillas

Nearly half the world's remaining mountain gorillas live in Uganda's misty forests.

🌊 Source of the Nile

Stand where the world's longest river begins its journey at Jinja.

🦁 Big Game Safaris

Lions, elephants, hippos, and over 1,000 bird species across ten national parks.

🌍 Warm Hospitality

Ugandans are renowned as some of the friendliest people in Africa.
