Uganda Attractions Guide - Site Plan

WDD 131 Final Project Planning Document

Site Name

Uganda Attractions Guide

This name clearly identifies the purpose of the website. It will provide information about Uganda's tourist attractions, wildlife, national parks, cultural sites, and travel destinations.

Optional Domain: ugandaattractionsguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help visitors discover Uganda's top attractions and travel destinations. The website will provide information about national parks, wildlife, waterfalls, mountains, lakes, and cultural experiences. Visitors will be able to learn about destinations and plan future trips.

Scenarios

  • What are the best national parks to visit in Uganda?
  • Which tourist attractions are suitable for family visits?
  • Where can I find information about wildlife safaris in Uganda?
  • What are the most popular attractions for first-time visitors?
